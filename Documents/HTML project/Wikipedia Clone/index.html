Roc (mythology)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Roc is an enormous legendary bird of prey in the popular mythology of the Middle East

The Roc appears in Arabic geographies and natural history, popularized in Arabian fairy tales and sailors' folklore. Ibn Battuta tells of a mountain hovering in the air over China Seas, which was the Roc.[1] The story collection One Thousand and One Nights includes tales of Abd al-Rahman and Sinbad the Sailor, both of which include the Roc.
